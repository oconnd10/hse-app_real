Statement of Purpose and Limitations

These guidelines relate only to common conditions or uncommon but life-threatening conditions. Dosages and dose intervals as specified are for adults with normal renal and hepatic function. Additionally, the recent use of antibiotics may alter the bacterial flora such that infecting organisms will not be amenable to recommended therapy. In these circumstances or if in doubt either take advice from a more senior colleague or contact the Consultant Microbiologist.
