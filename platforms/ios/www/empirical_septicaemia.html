Septicaemia

Clinical Condition: Community-acquired, , (excluding abdominal or pelvic source) Likely Organisms: Staph. aureus, Strep. pneumoniae, Neisseria. meningitidis, Step. pyogenes (Group A strep), Occasionally Gram-negative bacilli.
Antimicrobial & Dosage: Flucloxacillin 2g iv qds
Plus
Cefotaxime 2g iv qds
+/-
Gentamicin 5mg/kg/day iv od
Alternative Antimicrobial: Penicillin allergy (non anaphylaxis):
Vancomycin 15mg/kg iv bd
Plus
Cefotaxime 2g iv qds
+/-
Gentamicin 5mg/kg/day iv od
Comments: Perform full septic screen. Always take blood cultures first (minimum 2 sets).
Refer to Connolly Hospital Surviving Sepsis Guidelines Seek advice from Consultant Microbiologist if in doubt or in setting of penicillin anaphylaxis
